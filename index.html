Our Story

Founded in 2015, Lali Design began as a small workshop dedicated to preserving traditional Oromo textile arts. Today, we collaborate with over 50 artisans across Oromia to create contemporary designs rooted in cultural heritage.

Each piece is handcrafted using time-honored techniques, ensuring authenticity while meeting modern standards of quality and comfort.
