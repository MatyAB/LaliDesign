From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015 by designer Alemnesh Kebede, Lali Design began as a small workshop in Addis Ababa. Alemnesh wanted to create a bridge between traditional Oromo textile arts and contemporary fashion.

What started as a passion project to preserve her cultural heritage has grown into a thriving social enterprise that supports over 50 artisans across Oromia.
