From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015 by designer Alemnesh Kebede, Lali Design began as a small workshop in Addis Ababa with just three artisans. Alemnesh, a graduate of Addis Ababa University's School of Fine Arts, wanted to create a bridge between traditional Oromo textile arts and contemporary fashion.

What started as a passion project to preserve her cultural heritage has grown into a thriving social enterprise that supports over 50 artisans across Oromia while bringing authentic designs to a global audience.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate Oromo textile traditions while creating sustainable livelihoods for artisans through ethical fashion.

Our Vision

A world where traditional craftsmanship is valued and cultural heritage thrives through contemporary expression.

Our Values

Authenticity, quality, sustainability, and fair compensation for all artisans in our supply chain.
